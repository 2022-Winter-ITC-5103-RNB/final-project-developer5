The Kathmandu Valley Tour

  • Overview
  • Itinerary
  • Cost & Other Details

Below is what a typical itinerary looks like:

Day 1: Travellers arrive at the Tribhuwan International Airport. Travellers are picked up and boarded at Hotel Himalayan View in Nagarkot which is a destination 1 hour drive from the airport. You will be greeted with incredible view of sunset across the Himalayan range from your hotel room. Travellers stay overnight.

Day 2: Early morning Hike, Sunrise View and overnight stay at Hotel Himalayan View.There will be an early morning hike arranged to Nagarkot tower, some 2-3 hours away ending in spectacular morning sunrise views across the Himalayas. Breakfast will be served at 10am after which you can spend the rest of the day lounging and enjoying the spectacular views of Mount Everest and Lantang Ranges.

Day 3: Bhaktapur Sightseeing and Overnight stay in the centre of Kathmandu. After breakfast, there will be trip to the ancient city of Bhaktapur showcasing the ancient and unique culture of the Kathmandu valley, and later to Boudhanath stupa, which the heart of the Tibetan community in Nepal. Visitors will be boarded in Hotel Shangrila in the Thamel district of Kathmandu.

Day 4: Mountain Flights, Sightseeing in Kathmandu.There will be an early morning 6:30am mountain flight from Buddha Air. The 1 hour flight will take visitors over the Himalayas, including Mount Everest. Breakfast will be served after which there will be tours to Durbaar Square, the old heart of Kathmandu, old bazaars like and the Monkey temple.

Day 5: Flyback home. After the early morning breakfast travellers will be driven to the airport to catch the flights back to their respective countries.
