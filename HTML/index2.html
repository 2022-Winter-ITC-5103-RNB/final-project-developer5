Detailed Itenary :

Day 1: Arrival at Cochin.

Arrival at Airport / Railway Station and tranfer to hotel for check in.

After Arriving there will be a breakfast and then after 2 hours we will explore the town by visiting few markets like famous spice and chinese market. Along with it visit the church and portuguese palace. At the end of day we will have dinner with the traditional Dance Performance.

Nightstay in Hotel

Day 2: Exploring Alleppey

Today we will travel to alleppey by bus which is 70kms from cochin. After Arriving we will visit bird santuary and then in afternoon we will board famous houseboat and explore blackwaters. We will see the daily life of vilagers living along the water by visiting some of them. We will have dinner on the boat while watching beautiful sunset.

Nightstay in Houseboat

Day 3 and 4: Arrive Kumarakom

AFter having breakfast on the boat we will leave for the kumarakom which is 90kms from Alleppey. We will enjoy beach and experience the marine life. The facility for Scuba-diving and other water sport will be available for the intrested people.We will be spending 2 days in the resort.

Nightstay at the beach resort.

Day 5: Exploring Munnar

After breakfast at the resort we will leave for the munnar which is 150kms from kumarakom. We will visit famous tea gardens there. We will also visit famous temples of south india in the munnar. We will experience the famous elephant bath here.

Nightstay : We will be travelling to munnar kovalam at night.

Day 6 and 7: Reaching Kovalam

We will visit the silent valley and caves in kovalam. we will experience the nature for 2 days as we aere going to spend the last two days in the Jungle. We will explore the jungle and watch the animals and learn about them. It includes the elephant , tigers and nilgiri langurs and variety of birds.

Nightstay in the jungle resort.
