Ahmedabad! City Tour

3 Days! In Ahmedabad | Top Ahmedabad Places to Visit In Three Days

Day 1:

  • Arrive at the Sardar Vallabhbhai Internation Airport.
  • Transfer to Hayatt Residency, 1 hour drive.
  • We meet you at the airport with yor name on paper/board
  • Short brief about the tour and transfer to Science City
  • Explore till the world of science till the evening
  • Overnight at Hayatt Residency. Enjoy the beatiful view of Vastrapur Lake from the top of Hayatt Hotel

Day 2:

  • Morning beautiful Sunrise and a healthy breakfast made by professional Chefs at Hayatt.
  • Day trip, explore the Sabarmati Ashram, Riverfront
  • Evening, Witness the beauty of Sidi Saiyyedd Mosque.
  • On your way back to Ahmedabad, stop at Gordhan Thaal to have the best Gujarati Thali

Day 3:

  • Take a heritage walk in morning from Kalupur Temple.
  • After that visiting the Vintage Car Museum
  • And from there transfer to Ramada Resort
  • Enjoy the evening and an overnight stay at Hotel
