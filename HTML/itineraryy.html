ITINERARY DETAILS

DAY 1

Pick-up @ 1PM From the Mumbai International Airport - Terminal 2, Hotel VITS Mumbai houses a Poolside Restaurant, Ayurvedic and Thai Spa, Indoor Swimming Pool, Fitness Centre and Q Bar.
Located close to the Mumbai International Airport in Andheri East, VITS Mumbai Hotel is known for its plush interiors and quality service. Stay at the hotel, enjoying eminities provided by hotel management which indulged with LUNCH AND DINNER the day you have Arrived.

DAY 2

Breakfast at hotel, Later Proceed to visit Gateway of India, Prince of wales, Dhobi Ghat, Victoria Terminus, Raja Bhai clock tower , Sidhivinayak Temple Transfer to hotel, Rest of the day relax, Overnight at Mumbai

DAY 3

Breakfast at hotel then proceed for Dabbawala tour in first half and Bollywood tour in second half. Dabbawala begins at 10:00 and finished at 12:30 the word dabbawala implies a person in the Indian city of Mumbai who carries and delivers freshly made food from home in lunch boxes to office workers.
Afternoon proceed for Bollywood Tour in which you get know about origin and evolution of Indian Film chance to watch shootings of Feature films, T.V serials or Ad Films, Apart from this it give you glimpse of the most famous Film Star Houses (from outside) located on Juhu and Bandra.
Post tour drop back to hotel, Overnight at Mumbai.

DAY 4

Breakfast at hotel then proceed for Elephanta caves excursions. Elephanta is island located 10km far from city. DAY 5

Breakfast at hotel, VITS would drop you at Mumbai Airport. Tour Terminates.
