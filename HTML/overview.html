Kathmandu valley is home to ancient and medieval cultures and is the capital of Nepal. According to legends, the valley was once a lake and drained by the mythical figure of Manjushree. The mountain ranges, that ring the city are the mighty Himalayas, add additional lustre and mystique to this ancient city.

Kathmandu Valley tour will showcase both the natural beauty and the unique local culture. We have itineraries that meet the need of every traveller, whether you are looking for a short trip before transitioning to Tibet and Bhutan or looking longer stays.

Activities to do:

  • Everest sightseeing flight tour
  • Two nights at Club Himalaya Nagarkot
  • Chitwan Safari Tour
  • Two nights at Kathmandu with cultural sightseeing

Nightlife:

Kathmandu's nightlife is majorly concentrated in the Thamel region with varied options of bars, nightclubs, casinos and other places to choose from. These clubs are jam-packed with locals and tourists who are swaying to the lively tunes playing in the background. Some of the best places to visit in Kathmandu during the night are:

  • Club Dejavu
  • Kathmandu Durbar Square
  • Thamel Street
  • Jatra
  • Tamas Spa Lounge
  • Le Sherpa Restaurant
